Who We Are

Our Hometown, Our Home

Established in 2015 (Reg. No. MPM/CA/52/2015), the Chaliyar Residence Association brings together families from Karad Paramba — a peaceful neighbourhood shaped by the Chaliyar river, lush greenery, and the warmth of Malabar hospitality.

Formed to strengthen bonds among residents, we coordinate community events, maintain shared spaces, and support one another through everyday life and special occasions alike.

  • Safe, well-maintained residential environment
  • Cultural celebrations and festival gatherings
  • Neighbourhood welfare and mutual support
  • Youth engagement and senior care initiatives

Our Vision

To build a harmonious, inclusive community where every resident of Karad Paramba feels at home — connected to our roots and committed to a shared future.

Our Mission

Foster unity, preserve local traditions, and create a thriving neighbourhood through open dialogue, responsible stewardship, and collective action.
