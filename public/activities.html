Activities & Gallery

Our year-round calendar of gatherings and snapshots from life together in Karad Paramba.

Activities Gallery

Activities

01 January

New Year Gathering

Community breakfast, welcome address by the committee, and shared resolutions for the year ahead.

12 February

Health & Wellness Camp

Free basic health check-ups and awareness session for residents, arranged with local clinics.

10 March

Pre-Ramadan Iftar Gathering

Neighbours come together for an evening meal and fellowship during the holy month.

14 April

Vishu Celebrations

Traditional Vishukkani setup, kanikonna flowers, and a shared feast with all families.

15 May

Summer Youth Programme

Games, arts, and workshops for children during school holidays — building friendships across ages.

08 June

Monsoon Preparedness Drive

Clearing drains, checking common areas, and distributing safety tips before heavy rains.

20 July

Green Karad Plantation

Tree planting along lanes and common grounds — residents volunteer saplings and labour.

05 September

Onam Festivities

Pookkalam competitions, Thiruvathira, Onasadya, and cultural programmes for all ages.

12 October

Clean Karad Drive

Neighbourhood clean-up, waste segregation awareness, and beautification of shared spaces.

09 November

Diwali & Festival of Lights

Community lighting, sweets exchange, and an evening gathering celebrating togetherness.

21 December

Annual General Meeting

Review of the year, committee elections, financial report, and community dinner.
